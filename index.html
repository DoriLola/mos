About the Film

“I had the idea for the film for about a year now, but it didn't fully happen until I was in Berlin,” LOLA said. LOLA said that one of her favorite parts of the creative process is improvisation. “When I made the movie, I just assigned the characters, and then I told the actors to do whatever they wanted based on their character,” LOLA said. “So basically, none of it was scripted.”
